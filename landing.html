How it Works

You give us the keys to your social media and we'll handle the rest. Generic Corgi encrypts your login credentials, uses home-baked deep learning to understand your audience's preferred corgis, then auto-generates corgi content to your social media feeds.

Generic Corgi works with a large handful of social media networks, including Instagram, Facebook, Neopets, Geocities, and more. Let's see what Generic Corgi can do for your audience!
